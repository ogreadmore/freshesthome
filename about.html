Meet Taylor & Alisha

We bring high-end, eco‑friendly upgrades to interiors and exteriors that elevate curb appeal, raise property values, and reflect the resilience and character that make Cleveland home. Every project we complete is another house that shows what our city is about: pride of place, attention to detail, and a commitment to making things better.

Taylor Oliphant

I’m a 4.0 MBA graduate from Purdue with hands‑on experience as an elevator mechanic and years of home renovation work. I combine business rigor with practical craftsmanship to plan, execute, and deliver refreshes that make homes look new—and neighborhoods stronger.

Alisha

Alisha is a nurse from Nepal with a passion for organizing and cleaning closets—her favorite thing. She brings warmth, care, and precision to every project (and has a legendary shoe collection). Clients love how she transforms spaces to feel calm, useful, and beautifully put together.

Our family—the Taylor Oliphant family—has called Cleveland home for over four generations. Freshest Home is, at its core, a CLEVELAND PRIDE company. We built this business to help make Cleveland as beautiful as possible and to raise the value of houses and neighborhoods across the city. Raised property value, block by block—that’s the mission.
